Cheesy Pasta Recipe

Description

This is a dish that is perfect for the lazier or less accomplished chef! You don't need many ingredients and can be cooked in just ten minutes!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Seasoning

Instructions

  1. Bring a saucepan of water to the boil
  2. Add a pinch of salt
  3. Add your pasta (1 cup per serving)
  4. Simmer for 12-15 minutes
  5. Drain the pasta
  6. Grate your cheese and sprinkle on top
  7. Drizzle over some olive oil and season to taste.
