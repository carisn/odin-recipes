Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is a delicious and authentic Italian dish. The recipe is easy to follow, and will impress all of your family and friends!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Minced Beef
  • Passata
  • White sauce
  • Italian herbs
  • Mozzarella
  • Fresh basil
  • Onions

Instructions

  1. Brown off the meat and onions in a hot pan. This should take around five minutes.
  2. Add in the passata and herbs.
  3. Heat gently and stir until it starts to bubble.
  4. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Meanwhite, take a large ovenproof dish and snap the lasagne sheets to size.
  6. Layer a quarter of the tomato and beef into the dish.
  7. Alternate with the white sauce and pasta sheets.
  8. Slice the mozzarella on top.
  9. Place in the oven at 180C for 30 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and bubbling.
