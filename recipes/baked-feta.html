Baked Feta Recipe

Description

This is a TikTok craze that is worth the hype! It first appeared on the social media app during 2021 and was so popular that shops sold out of feta! Quick and easy to make, and utterly delicious. This recipe is a must for feta fans!

Ingredients

  • Penne pasta
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Baby plum tomatoes
  • Italian herbs
  • 200g Feta cheese

Instructions

  1. Place feta cheese in an ovenproof dish and sprinkle with italian herbs
  2. Add the garlic and tomatoes to the dish
  3. Pour liberal amounts of olive oil over the ingredients in the dish and toss to coat.
  4. Place in the oven at 180C for 45 minutes
  5. Meanwhile, cook the pasta according to the instructions on the packet.
  6. Remove dish from the oven and mix well using the back of a fork. Add some pasta water and mix until well combined.
  7. Add the pasta to the sauce and serve.
